Thank you for signing up with Knappsack! To get started please read over the Quick Start Guide

How do I build and deploy an iOS application?

Building and deploying an iOS application may seem like a daunting task, but don't worry, after reading through our iOS Build and Deployment Guide you will be an expert!

I've forgotten my login password. How do I reset it?

Don't worry, it happens to the best of us. Luckily we make it easy to reset your password. Navigate to the Forgot Password page and follow the instructions to have your password rest.

I don't want a specific version of an application available to my organization anymore. How do I achieve this?

We've provided you with two different options for achieving this.

First, you can flat out delete the application version. This will completely delete the application version and all its resources out of Knappsack. Once deleted it is gone for good.

Second, you can set the state of the application verion to disabled. Disabling the application does not delete the application. It simply makes the version unavailable to everyone. The application version state can be changed at any time.

Is there any way to invite more than one person at a time to my Knappsack organization?

Yes, we support batch invites from Gmail and Outlook. From either Gmail or Outlook, export the desired contacts to a CSV and at the Knappsack invite page you will see options to import from your CSV.

Can I change the credit card information associated with my organization?

Yes, you can change the credit card information for your organization at any time.

  1. While logged in as an Organization Administrator, click on the Manage Application link in the header
  2. Click on Organizations in the side menu
  3. Click edit beside the organization which you want to update the credit card information
  4. On the Update Organization page click the Organization dropdown tab and select Account Management
